# MTPE Analysis: Gaiden 3 - Deep Danger Situation (Parts 1-4) (深险之局)

## 1. Module A: Lore-Scan (Core Entities)

- **Zero (零/Zero)**: Main perspective. Network spy.
- **Jesmon (杰斯兽)**: Base form.
- **Jesmon GX (杰斯兽 GX)**: High-level evolution with Gankoomon's data.
- **Gankoomon (顽固兽)**: Mentor of Zero.
- **Sistermon Noir/Blanc (修女兽·漆黑/雪白)**: Human girls playing as Digimon.
- **Judith (朱迪斯/剑皇兽)**: Zero's rival/partner.
- **Zachary Lopez (扎克里/芳香兽)**: Treacherous superior.
- **Defence Minister (国防部长/颅骨兽)**: Corrupt official.

## 2. Module B: MTPE Analysis Details

- **Tone**: Gritty, spy-thriller style, emotional during the "death" of Gankoomon.
- **Terminology**:
  - "Zero Domain" (零域)
  - "Saint Fist Annihilation" (圣拳灭破)
  - "Endless Gauntlet" (无尽手套)
  - "Infinite Blade" (我为人人/All for One)
- **Logic Verification**: The transition from the "betrayal" in the real world (EDEN) to the Digimon battle must be seamless.
