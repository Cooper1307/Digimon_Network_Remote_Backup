### Paragraph 2: He chuckled, his fingers tightening around my throat as he hoisted me off the ground once more. He leaned in, his tongue flicking out for a disgusting lick against my cheek

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Fingers tightening around my throat"**: 替代 "grabbed my neck"。细化动作过程，增加读者的呼吸紧迫感。
  - **"Flicking out for a disgusting lick"**: “伸出舌头恶心地舔了一口”。"Flick out" 描写了猴类/兽类舌头的灵动，增加了生理上的厌恶感。

### Paragraph 6 (The Finishing Move): He roared the name of his finishing move: **"Deadly Ray Thunderbolt!"**

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Deadly Ray"**: 核心翻译考量。为了保留原文“死光”的杀意，同时向下兼容官方的 "Dark Spirits Deluxe" 基础设定的部分语义。

### Paragraph 7: The pressure soon pierced my skin, shattering the **Trial Badge** mounted beneath my collarbone. My body began to digitize. In that moment, the pain vanished, replaced only by a strange, hollow sense of relief

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Shattering the Trial Badge"**: “击碎体验勋章”。对应前文设定。
  - **"Hollow sense of relief"**: “空虚的解脱感”。这是一种斯德哥尔摩式或受难者式的心理描写，让死亡（数据化）变得带有一种诡异的美感和解脱感。

### Paragraph 10: Seconds? Minutes? I couldn't tell. Then, a black disc whistled out of the forest shadows... richness ricocheted off a tree trunk

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Black disc whistled"**: “黑色圆盘呼啸而出”。"Whistled" 传达了极高的速度。
  - **"Ricocheted"**: “跳弹/反弹”。这是一个专业的弹道学术语，表现出黑战暴丢盾动作的精准与力量。

### Paragraph 11: "**Brave Shield**"... this shield was different—it bore the unmistakable engraving of the **Crest of Courage**

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Unmistakable engraving"**: “确凿无疑的刻印”。
  - **"Crest of Courage"**: 官方术语（勇气徽章）。这里重点突出了“出现在不该出现的位置”这一悬念感。

### Paragraph 13: Reality hit him harder. He fell like a stone, and as he hit the ground, I saw it: a gaping, jagged hole torn clean through his "indestructible" metal body

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Fell like a stone"**: 对应 MetalEtemon 失去动力后的重力。
  - **"Gaping, jagged hole"**: “狰狞的、锯齿状的空洞”。描写了战斗成果的惨烈。
  - **"Torn clean through"**: “彻底撕穿”。展现了黑战暴在物理攻击上的绝对统治力。

### Paragraph 14-16: "**Owl**," he replied. "Are you okay?" ...He approached me... like a knight coming to his queen's rescue

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Dark silhouette flickered"**: “黑暗的轮廓在闪动”。
  - **"Knight coming to his queen's rescue"**: 这是对这段氛围的定性翻译。将黑战暴的行为上升到“骑士救美”的高度，为后续两人的羁绊定调。
