### 2. Detailed Material Explanation

**[Sentence 1]** ...发现自己误入了一座森林，并且还光荣的迷了路。

- **English Insight:** "Glorying losing my way" (光荣的迷了路) is a sarcastic/self-deprecating tone.
- **Translation:** Blissfully ignorant, I stepped through the data wall into the outer world. My attention was entirely fixed on my new self—until I realized I had wandered into a dense forest and was quite "gloriously" lost.

**[Sentence 2]** ...上面还在滚动播报...无非都是一些数码世界的社会性新闻...

- **Cultural Context:** Mirrors the "World News" scrolls in MMO games.
- **Translation:** Glancing back, the data wall was still visible, its surface scrolling with mundane social news of the Digital World. It was all the same nonsense: one tribe declaring war on another over a stolen turnip; another group swimming straight into a Whamon’s mouth; or a "rebel human faction" being infiltrated and pummeled by pro-human operatives in Digimon forms. Things that would be scandals in the human world were just everyday trivia here.

**[Sentence 5]** 那是一张相当猥琐的面容...完美的将 “油腻”一词表现的淋漓尽致。

- **Linguistic Principle 4:** Emotional Strength. "Younì" (油腻) is hard to capture in a single English word—it's a mix of "greasy," "unctuous," and "cringe."
- **Translation:** Before me stood a face that could only be described as loathsome. His teeth were a stained, sickly yellow; dark sunglasses masked his eyes; a WaruMonzaemon plush dangled from his waist. With a rose clenched in his teeth and a microphone in hand, he belted out a tune that was pure acoustic torture. He was the absolute embodiment of the word "greasy."

**[The Assault]** (Lines 228-229)

### 3. Vocabulary Deep Dive

- **油腻** (Yóunì)
  - **IPA:** /oʊl.i/ (Oily) / /ˈɡriː.zi/ (Greasy).
  - **Etymology:** Originally referring to fatty food. In modern Chinese slang, it describes men who are aging, self-centered, and lack boundaries/self-awareness (often "cringe").
  - **Word Family:** 油嘴滑舌 (Slick-tongue), 油腻男 (Greasy man).
  - **Mermaid Graph:**


- **化作数码残渣/数据化** (Shùjù huà)
  - **IPA:** /ˌdiː.dʒɪ.tə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃn/
  - **Context:** Deciphering the literal "death" in this world. "Digital residue" or "data fragments."
  - **Native Intuition:** It's not just "dying"; it's being **decompiled**.
  - **Example:** His code was **unraveled** into raw data. (他的代码被解构成原始数据。)

- **猥琐** (Wěisuǒ)
  - **IPA:** /ˈlʌθ.səm/ / /ˈpɜːr.vərt.ɪd/
  - **Meaning:** Loathsome, wretched, or perverted in appearance/demeanor.
  - **Example:** He gave me a **loathsome** grin. (他露出了猥琐的笑容。)
