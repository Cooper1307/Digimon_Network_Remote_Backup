### 1. Original Text, Summary & Oral Performance

> **Original Text (Lines 52-71):**
> 序章 攻略组
> 人类对于数码世界的探索永无止境...
> 现实中，人人都离不开网络。有了网络就需要维护...
> ...你怎么知道你派进去的那个人不会成为新的问题？...死在里头，化身数据，什么也不剩下。
> ...我创建了【攻略组】。每个国家各选出一名代表加入【攻略组】。

**Summary (主旨大意):**
The Prologue introduces the narrator as a cautious visionary who initially opposed the radical idea of "striking at the root" by entering the Digital World. After the tragic consequences of unauthorized exploration became clear, the narrator founded "The Raiders"—an elite, state-vetted unit designed to safely map the unknown and retrieve data samples. The text ends with a geopolitical snapshot of the "Big Five" and "Nordic Five" factions.

**Oral Performance (朗读指导):**

- **The Philosophy (Opening):** **Weary/Wised-up**. (一种见惯了生死、充满智慧却也疲惫的语气。)
- **The Argument (Paragraph 3):** **Aggressive/Sharp**. (反驳时的锋芒，语速加快。)
- **The Tragedy (Paragraph 6):** **Fading out/Heavy**. (描写“什么也不剩下”时，语速放慢，留白。)
- **The Welcome (Final Line):** **Cold/Ominous**. (最后的欢迎语应带有一种命运的沉重感。)

### 2. Detailed Material Explanation

**[Imagery: Shredding]** 死在里头，化身数据，什么也不剩下。

- **English Insight:** "To die there was to be shredded into raw data, leaving nothing behind."
- **Why:** "Shredded" (粉碎/撕碎) captures the violent deconstruction of code, perfectly aligning with the "Dark Fantasy" tone.

**[Geopolitics]** 【联合国五常】与【北欧五国】

- **Translation:** **The "U.N. Big Five"** and the **"Nordic Five."**
- **Context:** Using "Big Five" (P5) gives the unit an immediate layer of international political weight.

### 4. Native Speaker Perspective

  - **Note:** This is an inversion of the idiom "群龙无首" (a leaderless group). The English translation uses a more knightly, heroic phrasing to match the "Mega-level elite" vibe.
