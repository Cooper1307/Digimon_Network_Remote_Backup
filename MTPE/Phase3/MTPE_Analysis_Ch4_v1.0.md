# MTPE Analysis: Chapter 4 - The Dove and the Heron

## 1. Segment-by-Segment Breakdown

### S1: Internal Monologue & Relationship Hooks

- **CN**: "这段时间，我将之前发生在我身上的事情告诉给了枭..."
- **MT Potential Issue**: Too casual/flat.
- **AIE v3.0 Adjustment**: Enhance the weight of Sakura's trauma disclosure. Use "recounted the ordeal" instead of just "told."
- **Key Term**: 〖自然庭院〗 -> **Nature Garden / Nature Court** (Ensuring consistency with Phase 2).

### S2: Meeting Dove and Heron

- **CN**: "鸽是一名开上去比我小的男孩子，鹤是一名看上去比我大的女孩子。"
- **Linguistic Nuance**: The names **Dove (鸽)** and **Heron (鹤)** follow the bird-naming convention of the group (Owl, Falcon, Sparrow).
- **Tone Sovereignty**: Establish the contrast between Dove's "neighborly" warmth and Heron's "high-altitude flower" (高岭之花) coldness.
- **Mastemon (莫斯提兽)**: An Angel/Archangel hybrid form. Tone should reflect the "Dark Fantasy" aspect—graceful but dangerous.

### S3: The "Sparrow" Secret

- **CN**: "雀是他心里跨不过去的一道坎..."
- **Logical Consistency**: This explains Owl's stoicism. The relationship between Owl and Sparrow (deceased) is a major character motivator.
- **MT Check**: Avoid translating "表白" as just "confessed." Use "confessed his feelings" or "declared his love" to match the mature tone.

### S4: Systemic Lore (DigiCore & Maintenance)

- **CN**: "临时添补数据...止血带."
- **Term Rule**: **Temp-Patch Data** (Official AIE Term).
- **Lore Check**: The 6-day repair rule for DigiCores establishes the "High Stakes" of the world. Damage isn't just a HP bar; it's a data-matching ordeal.
