# MTPE Bible: Gaiden - Raider Rescue (攻略组救援战) v1.0

## Module A: Paragraph Analysis & Logic

### [A.1] The Tool Persona (Jade)

**Source:** 因为我只是他们的工具，一个工具发出的抱怨向来不重要。
**Analysis:** Jade (BanchoStingmon) views herself as a political tool for the French government. This adds a cynical, noir layer to the "Raider" status.
**Terminology:** **Raider** (攻略组成员) is treated as a high-stakes, state-sponsored profession.

### [A.2] The Fall of Angels (Metal)

**Source:** 进化成了座天使兽：堕落形态... 失控的天使屠杀恶魔。
**Analysis:** Metal (GrandLocomon) witnesses the forced corruption of an Angewomon into **Ofanimon: Falldown Mode**. This highlights the "Dark/Mature" tone of the story where traditional icons of light are defiled and weaponized.

### [A.3] The Master-Disciple Bond (Dragon)

**Source:** 小王（Wang）... 彻底变成了一头王龙兽（OURYUMON）。
**Analysis:** Dragon (Dorugoramon) carries guilt over his disciple's permanent decompilation. Their relationship is the only emotional anchor for the "Invincible King."
**Lore Sync:** **Ouryumon** (王龙兽) is a "Legendary Dragon" type, fitting for Dragon's disciple.

## Module B: Vocabulary & Etymology

### 1. **Ordinemon** (Origin: *Order* + *De*)

- **Etymology:** Derived from the concept of "Distorted Order."
- **Lore Context:** A massive entity born from the fusion of **Raguelmon** and **Ofanimon: Falldown Mode**.
- **Native Intuition:** Represents terminal system failure. Not a "monster" but a "manifestation of despair."

### 2. **Doric Bash** (豪勇金属击) / **Multitude Luster** (多路喧嚣)

- **Terminology Alignment:** Standardizing Dorugoramon's skills for maximum impact.
- **Project Decision:** Use **Multi-Discord** (多路喧嚣) for an eerie, overwhelming feel, or **Doru-Din** for official alignment. We choose **Multi-Discordance** to reflect his "Rule by Power."

## Module C: Translation Principles (The UN Big Five)

- **Metal (Russia):** Cold, mechanical, heavy emphasis on "The General" and "National Interest."
- **Jade (France):** Elegant yet cynical, uses romantic motifs (wink, jewels) to mask her "tool" status.
- **Dragon (China):** Authoritative, uses "Master/Disciple" hierarchy, highly traditional but powerful.
- **Zero (USA):** Silent, a "Lone Wolf" modified by system hacks.
