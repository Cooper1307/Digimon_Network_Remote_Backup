# Lore-Scan & Terminology Extraction: Chapters 4 & 5

## Chapter 4: The Dove and the Heron (鸽与鹤)

### Characters

- **鸽 (Dove / Pigeon)**: MegaGargomon (圣加鲁哥兽), defense position. Kind neighborly boy in human form, berserker in combat.
- **鹤 (Heron / Crane)**: Mastemon (莫斯提兽), reserve position. Cold, dark-style.
- **雀 (Sparrow)**: Former support, Angewomon. Deceased. Sibling to Dove/Heron.
- **枭 (Owl)**: Mentioned relationship with Sparrow (表白/一对儿).
- **隼 (Falcon)**: CRES Garurumon. Former taxi driver in reality.

### Key Terms / Systems

- **临时添补数据 (Temp-Patch Data)**: Fixing DigiCore damage. Scarce/Expensive.
- **核心工房 (Core Workshop)**: Repair facility for DigiCores.
- **赫菲斯托斯工房 (Hephaestus Workshop)**: For repairing Chromes Digizoid.
- **适应性战斗训练 (Adaptive Combat Training)**: Sakura's training regimen.
- **混沌降阶 (Chaos Degradation / Chaos Flare)**: Mastemon's dangerous skill (forbidden for training).
- **神圣希望 (Holy Hope / Divine Hope)**: Heron's move (right arm).
- **黑暗绝望 (Dark Despair)**: Heron's move (left arm - crushes DigiCore).
