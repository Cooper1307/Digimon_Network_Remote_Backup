### Paragraph 1: My mother passed away unexpectedly, leaving me without my only remaining kin

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Passed away unexpectedly"**: 替代了 "died suddenly"。"Passed away" 带有对死者的尊重与哀悼，"unexpectedly" 则保留了突发性。
  - **"Remaining kin"**: “仅存的亲人”。"Kin" 是一个带有古典色彩和法律厚度的词，比 "relative" 更能表现这种血脉上的孤绝感。

### Paragraph 2: In her will—penned while she yet lived—she transferred her Midgard account to my name... hoping it would offer me solace in her stead, keeping the shroud of loneliness at bay

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Penned while she yet lived"**: 替代了 "written when she was alive"。使用 "Penned"（亲笔写下）和 "yet lived" 给遗嘱赋予了一种仪式感。
  - **"Offer me solace in her stead"**: “代替她给我安慰”。"In her stead" 是高级表达，体现了契约和传承的严肃性。
  - **"Keeping the shroud of loneliness at bay"**: 这是本作常见的文学修饰。将孤独比作 "Shroud"（裹尸布/阴霾），并用 "Keep at bay" 表达阻挡，制造出一种挣扎感。

### Paragraph 3-4: After saying my final goodbyes... I found a diary. It was a record of her past—most of it chronicling her adventures in Midgard

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Saying my final goodbyes"**: 对应“送终/道别”。
  - **"Chronicling"**: 替代 "telling" 或 "writing"。这个动词暗示了一种历史记录者的视角，将母亲的网游经历提升到了“回忆录”的高度。

### Paragraph 5: "Lalamon... Kiwimon... Lilamon..." I whispered the names one by one. From her beginnings as a Rookie to her ascent to the Ultimate level, the pages were a testament to her long struggle

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Ascent to the Ultimate level"**: 使用 "Ascent" (上升/攀登) 而非 "Level up"，强调了进化的不易与神圣性。
  - **"A testament to her long struggle"**: “她长期奋斗的见证”。"Testament" 多用于遗嘱或圣约，在这里强调这些文字是母亲生命的结晶。

### Paragraph 6: I inherited my mother’s fierce competitive streak... A surge of indignation rose within me. I decided then and there to find Snake and settle the score

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Fierce competitive streak"**: 替代 "like winning"。精准描述了那种骨子里不服输的性格特征。
  - **"Settle the score"**: 对应“算账/一决高下”。这是一个地道的、带有复仇意味的俚语，完美对应了女主进入游戏的最核心动机。

### Paragraph 7: I waded through a mountain of junk shrouded in plastic until I finally unearthed her EDEN sensory immersion rig

- **翻译理由与表达讲解：**
  - **"Waded through"**: 原意是“跋涉”，这里用来形容在堆积如山的杂物中艰难移动。
  - **"Shrouded in plastic"**: “裹在塑料里”。
  - **"Unearthed"**: “挖掘出”。赋予了原本是电子设备的 rig 一种“出土文物”或“尘封宝藏”的质感。

**XML Summary:**

- **Tone:** Personal, somber, transitioning to determined.
- **Key Technique:** Using "Legacy" language (will, kin, testament) to elevate a gaming setup to a family heirloom.
