### 2. Detailed Material Explanation

**[Rule: The Time Dilation]** 一天约等于现实中的一分钟，四年约等于现实中的一天。

- **English Insight:** "One day there is roughly equivalent to one minute in reality."
- **Logic:** This 1:1440 ratio is crucial. 4 years (Digital) = 1 day (Real). This explains how a protagonist can spend "decades" inside while only being gone for a week in reality.

**[Terminology: Midgard]** 人们在数码世界建立了一个全新的登入据点——中庭（MIDGARD）。

- **Native Intuition:** Midgard (Old Norse: Miðgarðr) is the world of humans. Using this name for the "human-controlled" zone in the Digital World is a masterstroke of Lore.

**[The Guilds]** Based on different types of Digimon, ten Great Guilds have been established...

- **Cultural Translation:** 〖自然庭院〗 (Zìrán tíngyuàn). Literal: "Nature Garden."
- **Refinement:** **The Sylvan Garden**. "Sylvan" evokes a higher fantasy register than "Nature."
