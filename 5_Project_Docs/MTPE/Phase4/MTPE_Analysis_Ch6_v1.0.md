# MTPE Analysis: Chapter 6 - The Chairman's Request

## 1. Character Dynamics: Flower & Sakura

- **Flower (BanchoLilimon)**: Her behavior is "nature-like" in its raw, unapologetic forwardness. She "gropes" Sakura's avatar (Rosemon) in a way that is overtly bold.
- **Sakura's Response**: Sakura isn't a victim; she's sharp and defensive. Her retort about "don't make it look bad for both of us" and the physical "poke to the navel" shows her adapting to the guild's rougher social edges.
- **Translation Tone**: The "groping" should be translated with a sense of "unwanted familiarity" rather than simple assault, reflecting the chaotic, lawless nature of digital avatars.

## 2. Technical: The Belphemon Problem

- **Belphemon: Sleep Mode**: This is a "safety hazard" being moved through a public space.
- **Linguistic Focus**: Use terms like "Critical Cargo" or "Hazardous Data."
- **The "Internal Maintenance"**: A cover story for the guild's panic. Needs to sound like corporate/government PR speak.
